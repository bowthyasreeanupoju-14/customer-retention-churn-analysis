# Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer subscription behavior to identify churn patterns, retention trends, and customer lifetime insights for a subscription-based business.

The analysis helps businesses understand:
- Why customers leave
- Which customer groups are at high risk
- What factors improve retention
- How customer lifetime impacts business growth

---

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Google Colab

---

## Dataset
Telco Customer Churn Dataset

---

## Key Analysis Performed
- Customer churn analysis
- Retention analysis
- Tenure analysis
- Payment method analysis
- Contract type analysis
- Customer lifetime evaluation

---

## Visualizations Included
1. Customer Churn Distribution
2. Churn by Contract Type
3. Churn by Payment Method
4. Monthly Charges vs Churn
5. Retention Rate by Tenure Group

---

## Key Insights
- Month-to-month customers showed the highest churn.
- Long-term customers had better retention rates.
- Customers with higher monthly charges were more likely to churn.
- New customers were more likely to leave during early subscription periods.

---

## Business Recommendations
- Promote yearly subscription plans.
- Improve onboarding experience for new customers.
- Provide loyalty rewards for long-term users.
- Create personalized retention campaigns for high-risk customers.
